Steve Nash

Steve Nash is a Canadian former NBA player who spent his 18 year career with the Phoenix Suns, the Dallas Mavericks and the Los Angeles Lakers. During his time in the NBA, he won 2 Most Valuable Player awards, 8 NBA All-Star selections, and 7 All-NBA selections. He is most known for his playmaking skills, ball-handling and shooting, and is regarded as one of the best point guards of all time and the best Canadian basketball player of all time. In 2020, he was hired by the Brooklyn Nets as the head coach.

His Accolades:

  • 2× NBA Most Valuable Player
  • 8× NBA All-Star
  • 7× All-NBA selection
  • 2× NBA Skills Challenge winner

His Teams:

  • 1996–1998 Phoenix Suns
  • 1998–2004 Dallas Mavericks
  • 2004–2012 Phoenix Suns
  • 2012–2015 Los Angeles Lakers
